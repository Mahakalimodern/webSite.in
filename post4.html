Students from Classes 5 and 6 of Mahakali Modern Public School had a fantastic day learning and exploring at Radha Krishna Mandir in Mahendranagar, Kanchanpur! The visit was an enriching experience that combined education with cultural awareness. The journey to the mandir was filled with excitement. Upon arrival, students were awestruck by the temple's intricate architecture and serene ambiance. They learned about the history and significance of Radha Krishna Mandir from the temple priests and guides, who provided insightful explanations about religious practices and cultural traditions.

The students participated in a brief prayer session, observing rituals and listening to chants, which deepened their understanding of the spiritual aspects of their culture. A cultural program followed, where students sang devotional songs and performed traditional dances, adding joy to the day's learning experience.

The visit concluded with a group photo in front of the mandir, capturing the essence of the day. The students returned to school with a greater appreciation for their religion and culture, and a renewed sense of curiosity and respect for their heritage.
